What is an MBTI test?

The college calculator uses your personality type to help tailor which college matches you. Each section represents how your personality guides you through thinking. It is important to know your MBTI type because it will help you to know which subject are you better at.

E

Extraversion

This preference is for people who like getting their energy from being involved in events and participating in many activities.

S

Sensing

This preference is for people who enjoy using their senses, create experiences, and need something concrete to retain memories.

T

Thinking

This preference is for people who like thinking in a straight-forward and logical way, not letting their personal emotions have any influence.

J

Judging

This preference is for people who like to use their judging in their outer life, living in a planned, coordinated, and controllable manner.

I

Introversion

This preference is for people who like getting their energy through reflection and activities done alone or with one or two people.

N

Intuition

This preference is for people who like to think through problems and analyze problems.

F

Feeling

This preference is for people who make decisions based off the opinions of everyone involved and what maintains harmony.

P

Perceiving

This preference is for people who prefer to adapt and live flexibly. These people are spontaneous
